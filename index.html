Discount Calculator

Restaurant's Daily Offers and Discounts Calculator

This app allows the user to select the current day, quantities of drink and food items categorized by its prices and also if the customers has discountable cards like student's NUS or NHS/Police/Fire-dept workers. When the user applies for the discounted price pressing the calculation button it calculates the final price, amount discounted and initial price according the daily offer requirements and the customer's discount cards.

  • Asp.NET
  • HTML/CSS
  • Bootstrap
  • Jquery
